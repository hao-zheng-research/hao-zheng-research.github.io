Hao Zheng

I am a PhD candidate at the Department of Mechanical and Mechatronics Engineering, the University of Auckland, supervised by Professor Xun Xu.
My current research interests lie in the intersection of AI and Smart Manufacturing, focusing on the topics of Video Understanding (Action Recognition&Segmentation), Human-Robot Collaboration, Human-Centric Manufacturing, Generative AI.
If you're interested in collaborating on research projects related to AI and Manufacturing, feel free to contact me.
