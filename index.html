About Me

Hello, I am Hao Zheng, a PhD candidate at the University of Auckland specializing in human-robot collaboration, video understanding, and human-centric manufacturing. I am passionate about research on action recognition, multi-modal large language models, and generative AI. I have contributed to several academic publications and projects in these fields.
